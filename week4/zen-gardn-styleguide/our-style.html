Theme: Camping & Mountains

Our Zen-garden will have a camping theme. Our homepage will have an inviting night mountain scene with a campsite nestled within. We will create our camp scene with an illustrated SVG file and use other SVG graphics of animals and camping gear throughout the page. Our color scheme is complimentary with various blues and oranges.
